Indoors vs. Outdoors

Indoors:


Reasons why to play indoor also known as CQB (Close Quaters Combat)

  • The weather won’t have an effect on the game.
  • It is more close up so it is easier to get your target and also there is less exercise.
  • It is much more intense the less room there is and the more people in one space.
  • You don't have to worry about clothes getting dirty.

Oudoors:


Reasons why to play outdoor

  • It won’t be too warm in the summer.
  • You can use bigger replicas e.g. snipper riffles.
  • You have an excuse run around in the muck.
  • If you like exercise there is more running.
