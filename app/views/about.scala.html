About Airsoft Ireland

Airsoft Ireland was created in 2016 by airsofters, for airsofters to inform the community about everything airsoft in ireland from all known airsoft retailers in ireland, brands and arenas.

Airsoft is a rapidly growing sport within ireland, with new arenas and retailers opening every month, this growth has inspired many to set up websites for individual retailers and arenas.

We at Airsoft Ireland aim to bring all of this collective information into one place to further inform those inside and outside of our community.
