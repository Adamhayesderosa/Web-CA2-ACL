Irish airsoft retailers

  2. Irish airsoft retailers

Hobby Airsoft Dublin

Hobby airsoft is a popular walk in airsoft retailer focusing on entry level replicas and intermediate equipment, they have a small selection of replicas but make up for it with a vast array of parts and accessories, helpful staff that offers a repair service and reliable advice for anyone who wishes to know more about airsoft.

Main Irish Airsoft

Main Irish airsoft is a well-established airsoft retailer that started the boom of airsoft in Ireland, they focus on entry level and intermediate replicas and repairs. Main Irish airsoft specialise in World War 2 re-enactments for airsoft for historical purposes and sell many high quality World War 2 replicas and uniforms

Airsoft Eire

Airsoft Eire are a very professional airsoft retailer, focusing on selling the highest end products available to us in Ireland, they have a very large selection of absolutely anything airsoft from the starting player to the most advanced, and while they can be slightly more expensive to purchase replicas from, all products are thoroughly tested before sold ensuring high quality

SG airsoft

SG airsoft is a walk in airsoft retailer, that stocks intermediate replicas and equipment at a very competitive price, they have a large store that offers repairs and maintenance, SG airsoft also offer classic replicas from the american civil war for re-enactments

Airsoft Bay

Airsoft Bay is a newly established airsoft retailer that at this time is only online, they have a large stock on entry level equipment and have a few niche items that cannot be found anywhere else.

AirsoftGuns

AirsoftGuns is the only airsoft retailer in limerick, they stock anything and everything, however are slightly priced higher but all items are tested thoroughly before sale ensuring a high quality product.

Jokers of Airsoft

Jokers of airsoft is the only airsoft retailer in Kerry, and they specialise on intermediate quality Russian equipment, highly recommended for anyone wishing to view or purchase Russian replicas.

ProTac

ProTac in Kildare offer top class military equipment ranging from uniforms to overnight equipment, ProTac have been known as the go to place for the highest quality uniforms and clothes for airsofters, anything you could ever possibly need for you or team is available here

Munster Airsoft

Muster airsoft specialises in intermediate replicas is one of the largest retailers of airsoft equipment and replicas in Cork, offering a range of services from painting, decals, and repairs
